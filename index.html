Mighty Minis of Central Indiana

Mighty Minis is a sports enrichment program based in Central Indiana that collaborates with schools to provide a fun and engaging introduction to sports for children ages 1–10. Our program makes real exercise. Research shows that increased physical activity in children has significant physical, cognitive, social, and emotional benefits that support their growth. Mighty Minis brings a sports and physical education program to your child's school that both kids and adults love.

Why choose Mighty Minis?
